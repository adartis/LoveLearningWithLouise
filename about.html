Welcome to Love Learning with Louise

Where every child's potential is fostered for success. Louise is a leading educator with over 15 years of experience in and out of the classroom. She is a renowned inquiry educator and trainer and has earned the reputation for delivering learning with the best combination of kindness and rigour to ensure her students make the most progress possible.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower students to achieve their full potential through innovative and engaging educational practices. We believe in creating a nurturing and challenging environment that encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and a love for learning.

Our Approach

We use a student-centered approach that focuses on inquiry-based learning. This method encourages students to ask questions, explore, and discover new knowledge. Our curriculum is designed to be flexible and adaptable to meet the unique needs of each student.

Meet Louise

Louise has dedicated her career to education, working both in the classroom and as a trainer for other educators. Her passion for teaching and her commitment to her students' success have made her a respected figure in the educational community. Louise's approach combines kindness and rigor, ensuring that her students are both supported and challenged to reach their highest potential.
